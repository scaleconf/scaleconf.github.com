Signup for ScaleConf 2012

Because you're an early bird, the fee for the conference is only R1000.00 via EFT*, or $130 via other payment methods. This offer is valid until 1 January, after which the price will be R1500 or $180. This conference is non-profit, and any unspent money will go to charity.

The venue for the conference is the beautiful Kirstenbosch gardens in Cape Town, South Africa. Attendees will be given a boutique picnic lunch to enjoy in the gardens.
