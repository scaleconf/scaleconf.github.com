What’s it all about?

ScaleConf is a two-day conference that brings together local and international experts to share their ideas on the problems faced by companies and individuals building and running services on the internet today.

The presentations will cover the culture and methodologies used to architect and develop strong foundations required to operate websites and services.

Who should be there?

You should come to ScaleConf if:

  • You are a member of a technical team that operates a website or service for your growing customer base
  • You are involved with a team that needs to start thinking in modern ways and dealing with problems that are going to arise as your service grows
  • You are interested in the latest technologies and ideas from some great international speakers
