Sponsorship packages

ScaleConf is organised by volunteers, but there are still huge expenses when it comes to putting together a successful conference. Sponsorship helps us to keep ticket prices affordable.

We've structured our sponsorship this year to make sure that you can build up the benefits in a way that makes sense for your company.

Moving to a three day conference also gives more flexibility to organisations who wish to maximise their exposure. As with tickets, options are available for just day one (business focused), days two & three only (usual technical) and all three days.

If you want to take us up on one of the options then please send an email to info@scaleconf.org.

Baseline

Our starting point for sponsorship, the baseline package provides its value by giving you exposure and presence at the conference.

Benefits:
  • Company name or logo in attendee emails
  • Company name in press releases
  • Company logo on website footer
  • Company bio on website sponsors page
  • Two conference tickets
  • 15% off additional corporate tickets
Costs:
  • Day One: R9,500 ($1000 USD)
  • Day 2 & 3: R19,000 ($2000 USD)
  • All 3: R24,000 ($2500 USD)

Level up

Sponsors with the baseline package may want to build their package out a bit towards a specific purpose such as recruiting or presence. Below are some options which can be added to the baseline package.

Spotlight

Put your company in the spotlight, and be seen at the conference.

Benefits:
  • Prominent placement and larger logo wherever used
  • Company Logo on the back of the t-shirt
  • Standing banner in main hall (to be provided, no larger than 3m x 2m)
  • Logo on Lanyard
Costs:
  • Day One: R9,500 ($1000 USD)
  • Day 2 & 3: R19,000 ($2000 USD)
  • All 3: R24,000 ($2500 USD)
We're going to limit this package to just three takers so that everyone gets their time in the spotlight.

Presence

Bring some of your engineers in to talk shop with our attendees.

Benefits:
  • Area at the back of the hall (3m x 3m)
  • Three extra conference tickets
Costs:
  • Day One: R9,500 ($1000 USD)
  • Day 2 & 3: R19,000 ($2000 USD)
  • All 3: R24,000 ($2500 USD)
Space is the limiting factor here, so we will only be able to squeeze five spots into the venue.

Coffee

Our attendees love coffee, and they love our coffee sponsors.

Benefits:
  • Coffee sponsor designation for the day
  • Mention and Logo on the back of lanyard inserts
  • Mention during announcements
Cost: R14,000 ($1500 USD) per day Includes coffee

Events

We like to keep everyone socializing and networking, so on the evenings of the 9th–11th of April, we will be giving sponsors the opportunity to host events.

Benefits:
  • Official event sponsor
  • Host attendees for dinner, and/or drinks.
Cost: R6,000 ($650 USD) and the tab.
We can also manage and organize the event for you, just let us know.

Adopt a Student

Cape Town is full of smart but penniless computer science students wanting to experience and learn from events like this. It is also such a great opportunity for them to meet engineers from your company, ask questions, and get involved.

Cost: R1,100 ($120)
