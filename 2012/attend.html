Signup for ScaleConf 2012

Tickets are sold out for this conference. Follow us on twitter to hear about future events!

Follow @scaleconf

The fee for the conference is R1250.00 via EFT*, or $160 via other payment methods (we've been fortunate with sponsorship and attendance and so we didn't need to increase on the early-bird price too much!). Part of every ticket will also be going to a charity (still to be decided - email us if you are really passionate about any charity in particular).

The ticket includes entrance, lunch on both days, great speakers, and the opportunity to network with others in the industry.

The venue for the conference is the beautiful Kirstenbosch gardens in Cape Town, South Africa. Attendees will be given a boutique picnic lunch to enjoy in the gardens.
