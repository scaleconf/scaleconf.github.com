Workshops

With such a strong technically focused conference, we have decided to once again run some free workshops alongside the conference. These are informal hackathon-style workshops where engineers from the sponsoring company will be on hand to walk you through getting started on their products or services.

This year's workshops are not yet confirmed, so check back again soon, or follow us on Twitter for updates.
